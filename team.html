Meet the team

Ajay Varadharajan

CEO & Founder

Ajay is an expert in sustainability and the circular economy with several years of international experience in strategy consulting. He has worked with some of the biggest players in a range of industries including automotive, food and beverage, textiles and electronics. With a specialized dual master’s in Sustainable Systems and Engineering, an entrepreneurial mindset, and solid experience in consulting, Ajay offers a unique combination of creativity in ideas, quantitative rigor and business judgement in helping you succeed in your organization’s sustainability journey.


While Ajay is most passionate about making sustainability work for businesses of all types, sizes and cultures, rock climbing and local microbrews come a close second.

Jack Clackett

CTO and big boss

Jack is a full stack developer, specialising in Ruby on Rails, and has built fully optimised web applications for telecommunications companies among many other personal apps. After completing his degree in Mechanical Engineering, he worked in the Oil and Gas sector for just over a year before deciding that a sustainable and circular economy is where his passion really lies. He has an entrepreneurial spirit and would consider himself a ‘start-up junkie’ and loves getting involved in small, innovative companies that have big plans!


Jack is a tech enthusiast, a ‘maker’, and has a hands-on approach to any problem. He enjoys playing music, free-running, a good book and all-things-electric; he is currently building an electric motorbike with George.

George Borg

Junior Intern

George is a Ruby on Rails web developer, with exposure to various other coding languages, and has built multiple web apps from idea to finished product. He also has a degree in Mechanical Engineering and experience in a technical design role. Passionate about sustainable development and design. He believes that a circular economy is crucial to the future of humanity and will be achieved through creative, intelligent and entrepreneurial solutions covering a range of issues.


In his spare time he enjoys coding, making things (virtual and physical), gymnastics, running and reading.

Advisors

Joost Brinkman

Joost leads the Sustainability Services team at Accenture in the Netherlands and is responsible for their Dutch environmental program. He initiated and managed the Amsterdam Smart City program for two years. He is also a board member of the Green Business Club, a foundation that accelerates sustainable projects at business center Zuidas.

Jan Ludolf Heeres

Jan Ludolf is a Senior Manager at PwC’s Sustainability & Responsible Governance team in Amsterdam, where he helps clients implement monitoring and reporting frameworks, identify and address sustainability risks and transition from GRI G3 to GRI G4 reporting guidelines.

Maurits Groen

MauritsGroen is co-founder and CEO of WakaWaka and Vice President of the WakaWaka Foundation. As a ‘serial sustainability entrepreneur’ in the Netherlands, he has set up several sustainability organizations and at the same time, he is engaged in publishing books and organizing events on the same theme. Maurits is largely responsible for building the brand WakaWaka.

Jan Hein van Joolen

Jan Hein has been involved in everything from the rat-race in the financial world at the top-executive levels, to investing in and mentoring start-ups and consultancies. He understands the businessworld through experience gathered internationally and cross culturally over 29 years as a top-executive in the financial services industry. Jan Hein is currently active as independent strategic consultant for companies of all sizes. Apart from investing in companies, he enjoys encouraging/mentoring the new generation of entrepreneurs with a clear vision that resonate with his own.

Partners

Facilitated by Impact Hub, Green Insights is connected to the worldwide network of Impact Hub community, and has access to the local network and expertise of serial entrepreneurs, business leaders and mentors as well.


“Impact Hub revolves around the power of innovation through cooperation. We believe that there is not only shortage of good ideas to solve the issues of our time, but there is an acute lack of infrastructure to go through barriers and make them come true. Impact Hub was established to meet this need. Impact Hub is a well-developed entrepreneurial ecosystem for social innovation, internationalization and productivity, which contributes significantly to economic growth and solutions to environmental, economic and social challenges.”
